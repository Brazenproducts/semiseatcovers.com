Best Freightliner Seat Covers

Cascadia, Columbia, Century & M2 — custom-fit options ranked

Home › Top Picks › Freightliner

Freightliner is the best-selling Class 8 truck brand in North America — roughly 40% of all semi trucks on the road are Freightliners. That means more seat cover options than any other make, but also more junk to sort through. Here are the covers that actually fit Freightliner seats properly.

⚠️ Know Your Seat Type Before Ordering

Freightliner uses multiple seat manufacturers across models and years. The Cascadia alone has had seats from National, ISRI, Bostrom, and Seats Inc. Check the label on your seat frame for the manufacturer and model number before ordering custom covers. A "Cascadia" cover that fits a National seat won't fit an ISRI seat.
