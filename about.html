About Semi Seat Covers

Independent reviews for the trucking industry

Who We Are

Semi Seat Covers is an independent product review site focused on semi truck seat covers and cab accessories. We help long-haul drivers, owner-operators, and fleet managers find the right seat covers for their specific trucks.

How We Research

Our reviews are based on:

  • Material specifications and durability testing data from manufacturers
  • Fitment accuracy reports from verified driver reviews
  • Customer review aggregation from Amazon (verified purchases only)
  • Fleet manager feedback on bulk orders and long-term durability
  • Price-per-year of useful life calculations
  • SRS airbag compatibility verification
